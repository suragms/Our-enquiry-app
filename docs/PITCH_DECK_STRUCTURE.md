# HexaStack Investor Pitch Deck — 15 Slides

Use this structure in Google Slides, Figma, or Pitch. Each slide: title, 3–5 bullet points, design note.

---

**Slide 1 — Cover**
- Title: HEXASTACK
- Subtitle: Tagline (e.g. "Software for business and career")
- Design: Black or dark background, logo, minimal text. One accent (electric blue).

**Slide 2 — Problem**
- SMBs: Fragmented tools for billing, POS, inventory; compliance (VAT, multi-branch) is hard.
- Job seekers: Resumes fail ATS; opaque tools; privacy concerns.
- Design: Problem statements with short stats or icons.

**Slide 3 — Market Opportunity**
- India + Gulf + Global SMB and professional market size.
- TAM/SAM/SOM or addressable segments.
- Design: Simple market graphic or numbers.

**Slide 4 — Solution**
- One brand, three product pillars: HexaBill (business management), HexaCV (ATS resume), Hexa AI Tool Suite (career tools).
- Design: Three icons or cards.

**Slide 5 — HexaBill Overview**
- Complete business management: invoicing, POS, inventory, customers, analytics, multi-branch.
- VAT/compliance for Gulf; India-ready.
- Design: Product screenshot or feature grid.

**Slide 6 — HexaCV Overview**
- Free ATS resume builder; privacy-first; no login.
- ATS score, keyword gap, JD analyzer, bullet improver, section checker.
- Design: Simple UI mock or benefit list.

**Slide 7 — Hexa AI Tool Suite**
- Career optimization tools (ATS checker, JD analyzer, etc.).
- Design: Tool grid with one-line descriptions.

**Slide 8 — Target Market**
- India (SMB + professionals), Gulf (UAE, Saudi, VAT-focused), Global (English-first SaaS).
- Design: Map or three columns.

**Slide 9 — Revenue Model**
- Subscription (HexaBill tiers), freemium/lead (HexaCV), upsell (AI tools).
- Design: Simple revenue flow or pricing tiers.

**Slide 10 — Competitive Advantage**
- Integrated stack (billing + POS + inventory + multi-branch); privacy-first resume; region-specific compliance.
- Design: Comparison table or differentiators.

**Slide 11 — Technology Stack**
- Modern stack: React, Node, cloud, etc. Scalable and secure.
- Design: Logo strip or short list.

**Slide 12 — Roadmap**
- Next 12–18 months: product milestones, geography, key features.
- Design: Timeline or phases.

**Slide 13 — Traction**
- Current metrics: users, revenue, demos, pipeline (if any).
- Design: Numbers and short quotes or logos.

**Slide 14 — Vision**
- Long-term: default choice for SMBs and professionals in target markets.
- Design: One sentence + visual.

**Slide 15 — Team + Contact**
- Core team names and roles.
- Contact: email, website, calendar link.
- Design: Photos, titles, and CTA.

---

Keep slides minimal: title + bullets + one design idea per slide. Use brand colors (black, white, electric blue).
